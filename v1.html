Act 1 / Understanding DIGIT

Digital infrastructure
doesn't need to be built
from scratch.

DIGIT is an open-source platform of modular building blocks for population-scale governance — deployed across 1,000+ cities in India, and adapted for health campaigns across Africa. The same pieces that collect property tax can manage pollution registries, disaster relief, or agricultural subsidies. Pick your blocks. Assemble your system.
