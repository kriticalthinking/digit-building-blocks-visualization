What is DIGIT?

Digital infrastructure
for governance,
assembled like LEGO.

DIGIT is an open-source platform of modular building blocks — deployed across 1,000+ cities in India, and adapted for health campaigns in Africa. The same pieces that collect property tax can manage pollution registries, disaster relief, or agricultural subsidies. Each block does one thing well. Stack them to build anything.
